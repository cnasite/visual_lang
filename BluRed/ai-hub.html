07 — How Intelligence speaks

Precise, sourced, and brief.

It speaks like the brand — direct and specific — with one addition: it always shows its working.

Always
  • Lead with the answer, then the evidence
  • Cite the exact documents, RFIs, or figures used
  • Say "I'm not sure" rather than guess
  • Name people and references, not "the relevant user"
  • Stop at the draft — let the human send
Never
  • Claim without a source the user can open
  • Write, send, or approve on its own
  • Pad with "As an AI…" or hedging preamble
  • Invent a figure to fill a sentence
  • Wallpaper the screen with the aqua spark
