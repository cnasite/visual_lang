02 — The signal

One Neo Red per view.

Red is the colour of the decision — the primary action, the live alert, the mark itself. Used once and with intent, it leads the eye. Used everywhere, it leads nowhere.

Right — rationed

The single primary action carries the red. Everything around it stays navy and neutral, so the eye knows exactly where to go.

Wrong — wallpaper

Red on every button, border, and label. Nothing leads, because everything shouts. The signal becomes noise.

04 — The mark

The cube. Unchanged.
Used correctly.

The mark is the cube and wordmark together. White on dark grounds, red mark with dark wordmark on light, white on red. It does not stretch, rotate, recolour, or take effects.

05 — Geometry

Derived from the cube.

Three forms carry the logo into layout: the rounded square (the cube face), the forward arrow (motion, direction), and the 2×2 grid (structure). Corner radius is always 1:5 of the element.

Three forms, one logic. The rounded square, the forward arrow, and the modular grid define the Asite graphic system. Shapes frame, accent, and structure — they never fill space for decoration. Corner radius stays proportional: a 50px square carries a 10px radius.
