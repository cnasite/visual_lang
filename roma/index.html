01 — Logo

Monochrome body, one red cube.

The Asite mark, stripped of chrome. In the shell the lockup sets in ink and behaves like type, not an object. The cube's red is the same Asite Red as the product signal — logo and product share one chromatic identity, so the red cube is the only place the accent is permitted at brand level.

Wordmark is set wide-tracked uppercase in Inter (Helvetica Neue substitute). The ink lockup is the shell default — nav, footer, docs. The red-cube lockup is the master brand asset, reserved for deliberate brand moments: covers, favicons, hoardings.

02 — Colour

A narrow ramp and one signal.

Everything in the shell is drawn from a single achromatic ramp: four surfaces separated by hairlines, three text grays. One red — and it appears only inside a product frame. If a screen needs more colour than that, the screen is wrong.

03c — Illustration

Drawn with the same pen.

Illustration is the icon grammar at scale — four grays, one red decision. Line work explains; a single filled silhouette carries a brand moment. Never mix registers in one composition, and never more than one red decision per scene.

04b — Spacing & Motion

A 4px pulse.

Every gap is a multiple of 4. Components breathe in 8s and 12s; sections in 48s and 96s. Motion is precise and never theatrical: 0.2s for colour, 0.3s for transforms, 0.4s for layout — no spring, no bounce. If a measurement isn't on the pulse, it's a bug.

04e — Tables & Lists

Registers, not spreadsheets.

06 — Principles

The craft contract.

Four rules that keep this direction honest.

Ration the signal

Red appears once, inside a product frame. The moment two things glow, neither is primary. Status lives in dots, selection in a 12% wash. The shell has no colour at all.

Depth is light, not paint

No filled panels against filled panels. A 1px hairline and one soft shadow carry all elevation. Surfaces stay within a four-step ramp.

Density is respect

AEC professionals live in registers. Tight line-heights, 13–14px working text, properties inline — never make an engineer scroll for what could be a column.

The keyboard is the crane

Every action has a shortcut and shows it. ⌘K reaches everything. Speed is the feature that says we understand site-time.
